Exploring New Ideas in Unit 2

March 18, 2026

There were so many concepts and topics that I explored in unit 02. I personally feel that unit 2 is slightly difficult and more challenging as compared to unit 01. The number conversion and operations such as addition, subtraction were easy for me because I had learned it in the previous semester. I got a chance to learn about pixels and the RGB lights. How these three colors of light are used for any images.

I play with images and this was one of the helpful concepts I learned . I like to edit photos, while editing I use colour effects , adjust brightness level,blurring the image and so on,but I did not know the science behind it about the light intensity per pixel , the light range. After learning this it improved my editing skill better and I got to know why high pixel frames are bigger than low pixel frames in size.

The post in the Github account using Hugo and Gitbash was challenging for me. I did not understand it properly as another topic. How to use the git bash command. In lab session I solve many problems in this unit. Solving the problems is a better approach to test our study,how deeply we understand the concept . As an information science student it is necessary to understand the topic very deeply and able to solve question and keep trying if there is any error.
