Learn From Failure

This unit is very informative for me because it talks about failure. How they fail and work on their cause of failure. In this unit I learned that not every failure is the same , there are three types of failure. The 1st is Basic failure where we know the method but we do not care and our negligence also plays a bigger role in it. The 2nd is complex failure, here the situation keeps changing and more than one problem causes the failure, 3rd one is Intelligent failure where we try something new and an experiment fails. Great achievements do not come without failures. For example, heart surgeons also suffer from failures. That’s why now it is possible to have successful surgery and modern medicine.

The Learning Journey in Unit 01

In unit one of the course “Becoming a Reflective Information Scientist” I learned about DIKW model where D refers to data , I-information, K-knowledge, W-wisdom. This topic was not new for me , I heard these words before and many times we use this concept in real life without knowing this. Before learning this concept I thought these words information and knowledge are the same but there is a small difference between them. Data is the most basic level, it can be raw facts or numbers, it is not enough to understand. When the data is well structured with explanation it becomes information. After understanding the information we add our experience to make it knowledge and so on we take decisions on the basis of this, called Wisdom. When I learned this model I search some real life examples that help me to understand it better. I found a example and apply this concept trying to figure out the DIKW. I am in SR now my class starts at 10:45 am it was already 10:30 , I calculate the time there is only15 minutes left then i thought if i avoid lift and use stairs i could be there on time .Here, 10:45 and 10:30 is Data and left 15 minute is Information, Knowledge is understanding use of stairs instead of lift will save my time and Wisdom is using stair to reach in time . So this DIKW concept not only helps but also increases our thinking process.
