My BS Bot

April 16, 2026

I was really excited to explore chatbots in Unit 4, as they show how machines can talk and help humans. In this unit I created a chatbot for my university and named The BS-Bot. My chatbot helps the students who are interested in joining Azim Premji University as a student and very confused,with many questions on their mind.This BS Bot helps them by solving their queries and gives answers to their questions. It also tells why they should choose APU.While doing this project I am so excited and it was really fun for me and more engaging. I did not feel bored. It seemed like I am making ai bot like chatgpt. I feel that making a chat bot is so easy.

While doing this project at one point I felt challenged and failed to end the conversation with the user name that he put earlier. After a long time I was able to do it and go through many flows and objects and finally end the conversation very thankfully.

This was purely new for me. I had never done this before, no idea of this chatbot . I created this chat bot as a friend that gives answers very friendly and while talking you do not feel there is an AI or robot who is giving answers it feels like there is a person who is answering.
